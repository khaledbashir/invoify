Milan Puskar Stadium and WVU Coliseum I. PROGRAM PROJECT DESCRIPTION This project includes multiple components for consideration as part of a turn-key installation. a. Primarily this project will include the replacement of the LED Video boards at both the Milan Puskar Stadium South End Zone and the WVU Coliseum, inclusive of the video control systems necessary for the operation of the boards. b. Secondary consideration to include the replacement of the sound systems within each venue. The Stadium system being a single-point distribution on top of the existing video board and the WVU Coliseum system residing on top of the existing center-hung. c. Tertiary consideration to include the arena lighting within the WVU Coliseum, inclusive of game lighting, but also digitally controlled and motorized theatrical lighting. II. DESIGN SERVICES a. The awarded vendor shall be the primary contract holder for this project and act as the design-builder for this project. It is expected the contract holder will be the video board team with the sound and lighting as sub-contracts to the primary. The primary contract holder will manage and oversee the work of his sub-contractors, be responsible for the sub-contractor’s work, and general oversight of the project schedule. b. It shall be the responsibility of the awarded vendor to procure and/or provide stamped/signed engineering drawings/specifications by a WV licensed engineer, for the systems to be installed as part of this project. This includes the structural components of both venues, the electrical, lighting, video and sound systems necessary for the operation of the systems defined. c. Design professionals shall be licensed in the state of WV and have a valid business license. III. CONSTRUCTION SERVICES a. Contractors shall have a valid WV State and City of Morgantown Contractor’s license. b. Contractors shall bond 100% of their contract value. c. Contractors shall be responsible for all taxes; state and local. A. AESTHETIC CONSIDERATIONS
